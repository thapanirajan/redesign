Importance of Vegetables


Vegetables and fruit contain antioxidants and phytochemicals,
or plant chemicals. These biologically active substances can help
to protect you from some diseases. Scientific research shows
that if you regularly eat lots of fruit and vegetables,
you have a lower risk of: type 2 diabetes.
